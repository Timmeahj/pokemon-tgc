Pokémon Training Card Game

What is the game?

Each party receives 5 random cards. You and the computer. You use this to fight against each other. You do this by clicking on the cards. After you click, the cards are deployed and they fight each other. The one with the highest HP wins the battle but not the war. Each game also starts with 1000 points. Whoever is first at 0 loses.
