Pokémon Card Game

What is the game?

Each party receives 30 random Pokémon cards. You and the computer. Both are allowed to hold 5 in their hands. You use these to fight against each other. After you click them, the cards are deployed and they fight. Every time a Pokémon receives damage, the owner will also take that amount of damage. When the health points of that player (aka HP) reach 0 that player loses, the other wins.

The tactics

Pokémon TCG games are fast and furious. Here’s what you need to know. As previously mentioned you win if you get the opponents HP to 0. Your Pokémon will do this for you, but keep in mind that when they take damage you will lose your HP as well. This is why it is important to choose the right one!

Each Pokémon has 4 properties to keep in mind, those are:

HP, when it reaches 0 the Pokémon will be switched out.
Attack, the amount of HP points it can remove from your opponent.
Type, some types are strong or weak against others.
Weakness, this is the type to which your Pokémon is least effective.

As you can see it is important to keep all of those in mind when selecting your next Pokémon to enter the battle. 2 key tips to win your first match:

Take a Pokémon that can take a hit from your opponent.
Use your opponent's weakness to your advantage!
